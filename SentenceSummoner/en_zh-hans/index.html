How to Play

1
Summon：play a noun from your hand
「猫」→ Cat takes the field
2
Attack：your monster＋verb＋opponent
「猫咬龙」
3
Adjectives：buff yourself / weaken the opponent
「老虎强壮」「龙生病」
🎤 Goal：bring the opposing Summoner's HP to 0！
🛡️
