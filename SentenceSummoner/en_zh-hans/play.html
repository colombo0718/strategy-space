📜 Sentence Summoner

🎮 How to Play (type a sentence to act)

  • Summon：play a noun from your hand（e.g. 猫；one sentence can summon several, like 猫和狗）
  • Buff yourself：positive adjective + your noun（e.g. 老虎强壮 — stacks permanently on the monster）
  • Weaken the opponent：negative adjective + their monster（e.g. 熊猫生病）
  • Attack：your monster 攻击 their monster（猫攻击龙＝basic attack；use an attack-verb card like 猫咬龙 for bonus damage）
  • Defend：your monster + a defensive verb（e.g. 企鹅挡，temporary, stacks on the monster）
  • End turn：click the button, or type end
Hand auto-refills to 5 cards · Click a card to zoom in · Armor resets each turn, focus fire can break it
