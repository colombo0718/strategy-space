🎯 How to Play

Mages vs Dragon is a turn-based co-op strategy game: two mages team up against one dragon.
Each turn, both mages and the dragon secretly pick an action, then everything resolves at once.
Defeat the dragon to win. If both mages fall, you lose. Each mage has 10 HP; the dragon has 20.

🧙 Mage Actions

  • ⭕ Pass: do nothing
  • ⚡ Lightning: deals 1 damage alone — but 4 damage if both mages cast it on the same turn
  • 🛡️ Shield: one shield halves the damage (split between you); two shields reflect it all back
  • 💚 Heal: restores 2 HP to your teammate; if both heal on the same turn, each recovers 3 HP

Every spell must be chanted 👄 for one turn, then it is cast 👉 automatically next turn — no cancelling.

🐉 Dragon Actions

  • ⭕ Pass: do nothing
  • 🔥 Fire Breath: inhales 👃 for 2 turns, then breathes fire for 8 damage
  • 💥 Claw: raises a claw ✋ for 1 turn, then strikes for 4 damage

The dragon may cancel its wind-up at any time to feint, or switch to a different wind-up.
